My research

My interests are focused on three main areas:

Asymptotic Behavior of PDEs

Investigation of Moore-Gibson-Thompson equations and their applications in thermoviscoelasticity, including stability analysis and long-time behavior.

Machine Learning and Neural ODEs

Large-time asymptotics for hyperbolic systems with relaxation, employing algorithmic approaches for complex mathematical analysis.

Spectral Stability

Quantitative spectral stability studies for differential operators, with applications to domains with geometric perturbations and boundary conditions.
